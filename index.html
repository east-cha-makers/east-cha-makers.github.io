Inspiring Young Minds with Creative Technologies

East Chattanooga's very own maker space. Accessible, local and leveraging the resources and talent of the GigCity to inspire the next generation of thinkers, engineers and artists

Our Vision: By using technology we can break down the barriers to entry into a global creative community. Gigabit internet, 3D printers, LEDs, and credit card sized computers are the tools for 21st century ideas. We are a local idea factory where this new type of creativity can be made into reality.
