Technology Enabling Creativity

Everything we do at East CHA Makers, from 3D printing to minecraft, is designed to inspire fun while teaching critical skillsets such as programming, critical thinking and electronics.

Activities

These are just some of the activities happening at the space

3D Modeling and Printing

Everything from fun trinkets to useful objects. Not only do we 3D print but the children also use 3D modeling software to design their own creations

Minecraft

There is something immediately inviting about Minecraft. Open world, create what you want, build as an individual or as part of a group. Minecraft is the gateway to teaching children cooperation and programming

Electronics & Tinkering

Creating circuits, soldering and other fun electronics! Make LEDs blink!

Art, Fashion, Virtual Reality

Combining a variety of skillsets to merge art, fashion and tech.
